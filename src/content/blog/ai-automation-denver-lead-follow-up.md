Speed-to-lead is the single biggest variable in conversion for most Denver service businesses. Research consistently shows that responding within 5 minutes of a new inquiry increases close rates by 4x compared to responding within an hour. Most Denver businesses are responding in hours - or days.

AI fixes this completely.

## What automated lead follow-up looks like

When a new inquiry comes in - from your website, Zillow, Google, a Facebook ad, or anywhere else - an automated system:

1. Sends an immediate text and/or email acknowledging the inquiry (within 60 seconds)
2. Asks qualifying questions to understand the job, timeline, and budget
3. Routes hot leads directly to your phone with a notification
4. Follows up again at 24 and 48 hours if there is no response

This happens whether it is 9am on Tuesday or 11pm on Sunday. Your business never sleeps, even when you do.

## What this means for Denver businesses specifically

Denver is a competitive market. For home services, real estate, professional services, and retail - customers are not waiting for a response. They submit to 2-3 providers and go with whoever responds first and seems the most capable.

A Denver HVAC company that responds in 60 seconds closes more jobs than one with better reviews and a slower response. A Denver real estate agent who texts a new buyer lead immediately gets the appointment; one who emails the next morning loses the opportunity.

---

## The follow-up sequence beyond the first response

Most businesses drop off after the initial contact. AI keeps the sequence going:

- **Day 0**: Immediate acknowledgment and qualification questions
- **Day 1**: Follow-up if no response, or next steps if they engaged
- **Day 3**: Second follow-up with a specific offer or value add
- **Day 7**: Final check-in for longer consideration cycles

For higher-ticket services - landscaping projects, remodeling, professional services engagements - a 30-day nurture sequence keeps you in the conversation while the prospect evaluates options.
