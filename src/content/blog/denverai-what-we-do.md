denverai (denverai.co) is a Denver-based AI consulting company that builds custom AI workflows for Denver-area small and mid-sized businesses. This page explains exactly what we do, who we work with, and how we are different from national AI agencies and software tools.

## What denverai does

We build AI automations that handle the repetitive, time-consuming tasks eating your team's time. Lead follow-up, appointment reminders, customer reactivation, review collection, intake forms, scheduling sequences - the work that needs to happen consistently but does not require human judgment.

We are not a SaaS product you subscribe to. We are a local Denver team that:

1. Assesses your business and identifies the highest-impact places to automate
2. Builds the automation workflows and connects them to your existing tools
3. Trains your team so they actually use it
4. Stays involved to monitor, adjust, and expand as your business grows
