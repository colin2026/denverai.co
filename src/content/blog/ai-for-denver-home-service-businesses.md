Home service businesses in Denver - HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, snow removal, irrigation - all share the same operational pain points: leads that fall through the cracks, estimates that never got followed up on, and reviews that never got collected. AI handles all of this automatically.

## Lead response

When someone fills out your contact form or calls after hours, the difference between responding in 5 minutes and responding the next morning is often the difference between winning and losing the job. An AI that immediately texts new inquiries, asks qualifying questions, and books the call converts more leads from the same ad spend.

Denver homeowners looking for a contractor are not waiting around. They fill out two or three forms and go with whoever responds first.

---

## Estimate follow-up

Most contractors send an estimate and wait. The jobs that do not close are often just ones where the follow-up got missed. An automated sequence that checks in 48 hours after the estimate, answers common questions, and offers a call if they want to discuss converts a meaningful percentage of those leads.

This is especially valuable for landscaping and roofing businesses where estimates go out in volume and manual follow-up is impossible at scale.

---

## Scheduling and appointment reminders

No-shows and last-minute cancellations cost real money. Automated appointment reminders - a text the day before, another the morning of - reduce no-shows significantly. For service businesses doing multiple jobs a day, this adds up fast.

---

## Review collection

After every completed job, an automated text asking for a Google review gets dramatically higher response rates than manually asking at the end of a busy job. For a Denver contractor competing on local search, the difference between 50 and 200 Google reviews changes your lead volume entirely.

---

## Seasonal reactivation

Denver's seasonal service businesses - landscaping, snow removal, irrigation - have a natural reactivation window every year. An automated campaign that reaches out to last year's customers before the season starts, with a booking link, converts at high rates because the customers already trust you.
