Member churn is the defining challenge for Denver fitness studios and gyms. The average gym loses 30-50% of its members every year. Most of that churn is preventable - the members who leave usually just drifted away without anyone noticing. AI catches them before they cancel.

## Early churn detection and re-engagement

A member who has not checked in for 10 days is at risk. One who has not checked in for 21 days is likely gone. An AI that monitors check-in data and automatically sends a re-engagement message at the 10-day mark - with a personal tone and a specific offer - catches those members before they cancel.

For a Denver fitness studio with 300 members, recovering even 5-10 at-risk members per month changes the unit economics significantly.

---

## New member onboarding

The first 30 days determine whether a new member sticks. Most studios give a brief tour and hope for the best. An automated onboarding sequence that checks in after the first week, recommends classes based on their stated goals, introduces them to relevant trainers, and invites them to community events dramatically improves 90-day retention.

---

## Class fill rates

Empty spots in a class are lost revenue. An automated waitlist notification and last-minute class promotion sequence fills those spots without staff manually texting regulars. A message to members who typically come on that day but are not booked yet converts a meaningful percentage.

---

## Reactivation of lapsed members

Former members who left on good terms are the highest-converting leads you have. An automated reactivation campaign that reaches out 3-6 months after cancellation - with a "we would love to have you back" message and a limited offer - consistently brings people back at a fraction of the cost of acquiring a new member.

---

## Referral and review collection

After a member hits a milestone or posts a great result, an automated message asking for a Google review or a referral converts at high rates. Happy members at peak satisfaction are the best time to ask.
