The most common question Denver business owners ask before starting an AI project: how much does this actually cost? Here is an honest breakdown.

## The range

AI consulting for Denver small businesses typically falls into three tiers:

**Project-based work**: $2,000 - $10,000 for a defined scope. A single automation (lead follow-up, appointment reminders, review collection) built and launched. One-time cost, you own the system.

**Starter retainer**: $500 - $1,500/month. Ongoing management of 2-3 automations, monthly adjustments, support when something breaks, and quarterly expansion planning.

**Full AI operations**: $1,500 - $4,000/month. End-to-end AI operations for a business with multiple workflows, CRM integration, team training, and ongoing optimization.

These are ranges. What you actually pay depends on what you need, what tools you already have, and how complex your operation is.

---

## What drives the cost

**Complexity of the workflow.** A simple lead response automation is faster to build than a multi-step intake and routing system connected to your CRM, calendar, and texting platform.

**Number of integrations.** Connecting to software you already use takes time. The more systems involved, the more build time.

**Ongoing management.** Automations need maintenance. Things change - your software updates, your process changes, your team grows. A retainer covers that.

**Training and adoption.** The best automation is worthless if your team does not trust it. We include training in every engagement.
