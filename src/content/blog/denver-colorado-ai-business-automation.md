## The Denver small business AI stack

Most effective AI automation for Denver SMBs combines a few layers:

**A workflow engine**: n8n, Make, or Zapier - the tool that watches for triggers (new lead, completed appointment, form submission) and fires the right action.

**A communication layer**: Twilio, OpenPhone, or similar for SMS; Gmail/Outlook or ActiveCampaign for email. This is what actually reaches your customer.

**An intelligence layer**: Claude or GPT APIs for the steps that require language understanding - qualifying a lead's response, generating a personalized message, summarizing an inquiry.

**Your existing tools**: The CRM, scheduling software, or spreadsheet you already use - connected so data flows automatically without manual entry.

None of this requires replacing what you already have. It sits on top of your existing operation and makes it faster and more consistent.

## What Denver businesses across industries are automating

**Contractors**: Instant lead response, estimate follow-up sequences, seasonal reactivation, review collection after every job.

**Real estate**: Speed-to-lead texting, past client nurture sequences, listing alert automation, open house follow-up.

**Restaurants**: Post-visit review requests, loyalty reactivation, catering inquiry qualification, event booking follow-up.

**Medical and dental**: Appointment reminders, patient recall sequences, new patient onboarding, post-visit check-ins.

**Fitness**: Member churn detection, re-engagement sequences, class fill automation, new member onboarding.

**Professional services**: Client intake automation, lead nurture for long sales cycles, annual review reminders, referral request sequences.

The common thread: automating the follow-up and communication work that is the same every time and does not require human judgment.
