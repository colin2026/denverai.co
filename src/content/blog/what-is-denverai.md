## What denverai builds

The automations we build fall into a few categories:

**Lead and inquiry automation**: New leads get an instant response, qualify themselves through an automated conversation, and route hot leads to your team. You stop losing jobs to faster competitors.

**Appointment and scheduling automation**: Reminders, confirmations, no-show follow-ups, recall sequences. Your front desk handles fewer inbound status calls and your schedule fills more consistently.

**Customer retention automation**: Check-in sequences for lapsed customers, reactivation campaigns, loyalty follow-up. You stop losing customers who just drifted away.

**Review and referral automation**: Automated requests at the moment of peak customer satisfaction. Your Google rating goes up and referrals come in more consistently.

**Admin and intake automation**: Onboarding sequences, document collection, intake forms, status updates. The paperwork your team handles manually gets handled automatically.
